Polar Bears

The polar bear is a hypercarnivorous bear whose native range lies largely within the Arctic Circle, encompassing the Arctic Ocean,
its surrounding seas and surrounding land masses. It is the largest extant bear species, as well as the largest extant land carnivore. Due to Climate Change it is considered a vulnerable species
